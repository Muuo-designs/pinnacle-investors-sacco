About us

Pinnacle Investor Sacco is a youth-driven financial empowerment group founded on 20th May 2024 on the principles of saving, investment, and growth.

It was established to create a strong financial foundation for it's members,the Sacco provides a platform to nurture responsible financial habits and unlock investment opportunities.

We are guided by transparency,unity and accountability.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to building a financially empowered community by promoting a culture of saving, investing, and responsible governance. Our goal is to create lasting value for our members through trust, professionalism, and continuous growth.

Our Objectives

  • Promote a culture of saving among members.
  • Provide sustainable investment opportunities.
  • Support members' financial growth and stability.
  • Uphold transparency, accountability, and good governance.
  • Foster unity and shared prosperity within the SACCO.
