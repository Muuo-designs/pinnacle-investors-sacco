📝Registration Requirements

Become a member of Pinnacle Investors SACCO and start your journey towards financial growth.

Requirements

  1. A copy of your National ID or a valid National ID Number.
  2. Registration Fee: KSh 250 (non-refundable).
  3. Complete the Membership Registration Form.
  4. Provide an active mobile phone number.
  5. Provide a valid email address (optional but recommended).
  6. Read, understand, and agree to abide by the Pinnacle Investors SACCO Constitution before registering.

Declaration

By submitting your registration, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with the Constitution, rules, regulations, and policies of Pinnacle Investors SACCO.

Benefits of Membership

  • Access to savings and investment opportunities.
  • Eligibility to apply for SACCO loans (subject to the SACCO's terms and conditions).
  • Participate in member meetings and decision-making.
  • Earn returns on eligible investments in accordance with the SACCO's policies.

Join Pinnacle Investors SACCO today and build a secure financial future with us.
